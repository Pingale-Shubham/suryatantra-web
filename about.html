Our Journey Since 2019

Suryatantra was incorporated with a primary aim: to utilize state-of-the-art PCB Manufacturing, Drilling & Routing processes to provide exceptional quality to our customers.

Backed by two decades of experience in the industry, we understand the unique challenges of small, medium, and large-scale units. This deep insight allows us to design custom solutions for better quality and profitability.

Our founder's success with 'Surya Electronics' laid the foundation. Today, combining Suryatantra's latest technologies with our legacy of trust, we serve reputed clients like India Circuit Limited, Signum Electrowave, and Shogini Techno Arts.
